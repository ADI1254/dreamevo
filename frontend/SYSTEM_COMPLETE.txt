⚡ VOLUME DUCKING SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

HOW IT WORKS:

  SCENARIO: Narration starts at 18 seconds

  BEFORE NARRATION (0-18s):
  ├─ Orchestra: 40% ────────────────────►
  ├─ SFX:       25% ────────────────────►
  ├─ Voice:     (silent)
  └─ Result:    Rich orchestral layer plays

  DURING NARRATION (18-25s):
  ├─ Orchestra: 40% → 15% (DUCKED smoothly)
  ├─ SFX:       25% → 15% (Turns down too)
  ├─ Voice:     0% → 95% (ENTERS PROMINENTLY)
  └─ Result:    ✅ Narration crystal clear!

  AFTER NARRATION (25+s):
  ├─ Orchestra: 15% → 40% (RESTORED smoothly)
  ├─ SFX:       15% → 25% (Returns to normal)
  ├─ Voice:     95% → (silent)
  └─ Result:    Music returns naturally
